Voltage Warning

Please note that only certain openLRS units can be flashed with 5V USB-serial adapter.
It is strongly suggested to always use 3.3V USB-serial adapter.

Permanent damage is caused immediately by powering non compatible units with 5V.

Instructions

1. Connect your TX or RX module via usb to serial dongle to the computer (with DTR)
2. Selected desired firmware for your module
3. Press "Flash Firmware" and wait for process to finish
4. You are done

Note: Auto-Connect is always disabled in firmware flasher.
